- GOD IS LOVE -
I grew up into a religious family and at a young age. It is because of this religiousness that I was introduced to the idea of unconditional love, a concept that I couldn’t properly understand as a kid.
The Idea of loving not only those who are kind to you but those who are your enemies as well. Those who spoke against you, those who hurt you and also those who you hate, I learnt to love all of these people without expecting anything back.
- LOVE YOU FAM -
The well being of the family was always the most important thing between my siblings and I and my parents. Throughout the years they way my parents disciplined me gave me this feeling of hate towards them.
But as I matured and grew this feeling of hate turned into a strong love, care and respect towards my parents because I realized that I wouldn’t be who I am at present if they didn’t do to me what they did in the past.
- WILL YOU BE MINE? -
Going into a relationship was never really a priority to me. I could never really get the feeling of urgency that others have in trying to get into a relationship.
But If ever I decide to get into a relationship, I made a promise to be physically and mentally fit before I can accept another person into my life, After all how can I take care of someone else if I cant even take care of myself?
